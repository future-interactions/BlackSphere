Our design model informs how we approach our work, drawing on each method or process at the right stage of a project.

FI08: Design Model


The model is built around two complimentary modes — inquiry and speculation. Each share evaluation as an objective. We inquire to evaluate what's happening on the ground and we speculate to evaluate the implications of new arrangements, relationships or interactions on any given problem or opportunity space.

We do not believe that great design happens in a waterfall or in any prescribed sequence. For example, speculation does not follow inquiry — it supports it and vice versa.
