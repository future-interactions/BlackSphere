There is no singular approach to problem solving or to opportunity finding. Our design model and analysis framework inform how we shape each approach to the unique situations and contexts we are working in.

FI08: Design Model

FI13: Analysis Framework

Our design model is built around two complimentary modes—inquiry and speculation—and share evaluation as an objective. We inquire to evaluate what's happening on the ground and we speculate to evaluate the implications of new arrangements, relationships or interactions on any given problem or opportunity space.

Our analysis framework creates a common language for how we interpret the way action is distributed between people and things. It informs how we read, interpret, design for and measure changes within situations and contexts.
