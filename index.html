The Office of Future Interactions is an inquiry led design & innovation studio.
We specialize in the interpretation of shifting social, cultural and environmental patterns; deep expertise in emerging technology; and designing and assessing the implications of speculative near-future experiences — so that we can create meaningful and tangible opportunities for our partners.
